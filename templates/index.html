About Us

Welcome to IQRA SCHOOL

Welcome to the online IQRA School, where we offer an innovative and engaging approach to learning through the use of programming. Our school is dedicated to providing students with a high-quality education that prepares them for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and computational skills through the use of programming languages such as Python, Java, and JavaScript. We believe that learning to code is not only a valuable skill in itself, but it also helps students develop important life skills such as perseverance, logical reasoning, and creativity.

Online IQRA School uses programming to provide a modern and engaging approach to learning.

The curriculum is focused on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and computational skills through programming languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript.

Courses are tailored to students of all ages and skill levels, and cover a variety of subjects such as math, science, history, and language arts.

The platform is designed for students to learn at their own pace and from anywhere in the world, with personalized instruction and support from experienced teachers.

IQRA School prioritizes an inclusive and supportive learning environment that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing world.
